Our Story

Golden Crumb Bakery started in 2009 when founder Clara Whitfield converted her grandmother's farmhouse kitchen into a proper bakery. What began as weekend farmers market stalls quickly became a beloved neighborhood institution.

We believe great bread is honest food — flour, water, salt, time. No shortcuts, no preservatives, no compromises. Every loaf is hand-shaped and every croissant is laminated by hand.

Our team of five bakers arrives before dawn, six days a week, so that by the time you walk through our door the cases are full and the ovens are still warm.
